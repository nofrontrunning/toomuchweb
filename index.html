FAQs
How does this work?
TooMuch grabs the quote from your page when you are swapping, compares it with all major DEX aggregators via their API and show you the best prices.
How safe is this?
It is very safe. The extension does not connect to your wallets, nor does it have access to any transactions. All it does is grab the quote from your page and compare it with all major DEXs and aggregators. The codebase is fully open-sourced on Github, you can also inspect the extension code directly.
What do I use it?
Install and forget about it, the extension will automatically detect your swapping activity and popup with rate comparison before you confirm swap. For a quick test, go to uniswap and try to swap some tokens.
