FAQs
How does this work?
TooMuch grabs the quote from your page when you are swapping, compares it with all major DEX aggregators via their API and show you the best prices.
How safe is this?
It is very safe. The extension does not have access to any wallets and transactions at all, it simply just compare rates. The extension runs locally in your browser and the codebase is fully open-sourced, anyone can inspect it.
