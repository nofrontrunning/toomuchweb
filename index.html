FAQs
How does this work?
TooMuch detects and grabs quote from the web page before you confirm swap, compares it with all major DEX aggregators via their API and show you the results.
How safe is this?
Very safe. The extension does not connect to your wallets, nor does it have access to your transactions. All it does is grabbing and comparing quote. The codebase is fully open-sourced on Github, you can also inspect the extension code directly.
Is it free?
Yes. I might add a paid plan in the future, but basic usage will always be free. It makes me dance in front my computer every time you share and review TooMuch.
How do I use it?
Install and forget about it, the extension will automatically detect and popup with rate comparison before you swap. For a quick test, go to uniswap and pretend to swap some tokens.
What chains does it support?
Currently only Ethereum mainnet, Ethereum L2s and other EVM chains in the pipeline.
